How It Works

Each race, players place objects on the track: ramps, oil slicks, spinning hammers. They shape the chaos for everyone. Score points by finishing races, triggering your own objects, and outsmarting your rivals.

Time to go full throttle!

Race hard, overtake your rivals, and dodge every obstacle on track. Hit the boost pads, cross the finish line first and secure maximum placement points!

Time to weaponize the track!

Racing pauses but the chaos begins. Choose a trap from a random pool and place it anywhere on the circuit. Watch your opponents crash into your traps to score extra sabotage points!

Design Pillars

Controlled Chaos

Every race is unpredictable, but the unpredictability always comes from deliberate player choices, not pure randomness. The track transforms lap by lap by collective will.

Comeback Loop

Players behind in the standings always have concrete tools to catch up. No one is out of the game before the last race.

Split Experience

TV and phone are two complementary roles in the same experience. The shared screen creates social moments; the phone creates intimacy and control.

Our Story

Why We Built SaboTrack

We wanted to create a game that feels like a party from the moment you turn it on: chaotic, social, and always fair. SaboTrack was born from our love of party games and our frustration with games that leave players feeling powerless.

Apple Foundation Program

SaboTrack was developed as part of the Apple Foundation Program Avanzato 2026, an intensive program where selected developers build real products using Apple technologies. The experience shaped every design decision, from the asymmetric Apple TV + iPhone setup to the clean, minimal aesthetic.
